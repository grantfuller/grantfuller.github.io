Grant Fuller

I am excited to foster tap talent in Cache Valley. My parents started me tap dancing as a three year old and I have never stopped since. Recently I used my talents on the BYU International Folk Dance team in both group numbers and as a featured soloist. Also, I was hired by BYU as a tap instructor for beginning, intermediate, and advanced classes. I truly love to be involved in the learning process and to help students grasp new concepts.

Currently I am earning my Masters degree in Information Systems from USU and am a father to an extremely adorable one year old (who is already catching on to good rhythm).

Some of Grant's favorite steps are:

  • Pullbacks
  • Paddles
  • Georges/Georgettes
Classes

Classes

We offer both a beginning and intermediate level class

Beginning

This class with introduce a variety of beginning tap moves and techniques that will form a foundation on which to build. Come with little to no experience! We will cover flaps, shuffles, toe knocks, cramp rolls, and many other steps

Intermediate

This is for students who have a foundational knowledge of tap moves and techniques. In this class we will focus on speed and variation of sound. We will cover a variety of moves and their variations (riffs, Maxie Ford, time steps, and many others), syncopated and swung rhythms, and much more. Come prepared for a workout!
